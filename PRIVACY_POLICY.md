# Mindle — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2, 2026**

## Overview

Mindle ("the App") is a word puzzle game developed by Shan Virani. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

**Account Data (optional)**
If you choose to sign in with Apple or Google, we store:
- Your email address (for account identification)
- A unique user ID
- Your chosen display name

**Game Data**
We store your game progress locally on your device and optionally in the cloud if you sign in:
- Puzzles completed, words found, coins earned
- Daily streak and achievement progress
- Equipped cosmetic items

**Analytics**
We collect anonymous usage data to improve the App:
- Puzzle completion events (category, time, words found)
- Feature usage (which screens are visited)
- Device type and OS version

We do not collect or store your precise location, contacts, photos, or any other sensitive personal data.

## Third-Party Services

**Google AdMob** — We show ads to support the App. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad targeting. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

**Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In** — If you choose to sign in, authentication is handled by Apple or Google. We only receive the email and name you authorize.

**Supabase** — We use Supabase for cloud storage and authentication. Your data is stored securely in their infrastructure. See [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

**Expo** — We use Expo for push notifications. See [Expo Privacy Policy](https://expo.dev/privacy).

## How We Use Your Data

- To save and sync your game progress
- To show your name on leaderboards
- To send push notifications (daily reminders, challenge results) if you opt in
- To display ads
- To improve the game experience

## Data Retention

Your game data is stored as long as your account exists. If you delete your account (Settings > Account > Delete Account), we permanently remove your cloud data from our servers. Local data on your device is cleared when you uninstall the App.

## Children's Privacy

Mindle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us.

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Delete your account** at any time from Settings
- **Opt out of notifications** from Settings
- **Play without signing in** — an account is optional
- **Request your data** by contacting us

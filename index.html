Mindle — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

Mindle ("the App") is a word puzzle game developed by Shan Virani. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Account Data (optional)
If you choose to sign in with Apple or Google, we store your email address, a unique user ID, and your chosen display name.

Game Data
We store your game progress locally on your device and optionally in the cloud if you sign in: puzzles completed, words found, coins earned, daily streak and achievement progress, and equipped cosmetic items.

Analytics
We collect anonymous usage data to improve the App: puzzle completion events, feature usage, and device type. We do not collect or store your precise location, contacts, photos, or any other sensitive personal data.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — We show ads to support the App. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad targeting. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In — Authentication is handled by Apple or Google. We only receive the email and name you authorize.
  • Supabase — Cloud storage and authentication. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Expo — Push notifications. See Expo Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Data

  • To save and sync your game progress
  • To show your name on leaderboards
  • To send push notifications (daily reminders, challenge results) if you opt in
  • To display ads
  • To improve the game experience

Data Retention

Your game data is stored as long as your account exists. If you delete your account (Settings > Account > Delete Account), we permanently remove your cloud data from our servers. Local data on your device is cleared when you uninstall the App.

Children's Privacy

Mindle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us.

Your Rights

  • Delete your account at any time from Settings
  • Opt out of notifications from Settings
  • Play without signing in — an account is optional
  • Request your data by contacting us
